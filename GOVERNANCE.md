# Governance — Agentic DNA (aDNA)

## Founding Architect

Stanley Bishop is the Founding Architect of the Lattice Protocol. All technical
decisions, architectural direction, and protocol evolution are under the authority
of the Founding Architect as established by the Founding Architect Charter
(2026-03-07).

## Open Standard

The aDNA type system (Module, Lattice, Dataset) is intentionally MIT-licensed
as an open standard. This enables adoption and interoperability across the
federated AI ecosystem. The open license does not extend to the protocol
implementation (`lattice-protocol`) or product layer (`latlab`).

## Contribution Process

1. All contributions require approval from the Founding Architect or designated maintainers
2. Pull requests must pass CI checks
